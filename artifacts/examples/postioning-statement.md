## Positioning Statement 

A simple statement that declares who you're for, what you do for them, and why you're different from the alternatives. Keep it specific enough that it tells you what to build and what to decline.

**A simple format:**

_For [target segment] who [have this problem], [product name] is a [category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [alternative], we [key differentiator]._

You don't have to use a template for your statement, this is simply a guide.
