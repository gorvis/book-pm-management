### Segmentation

You can't serve everyone first. The quadrant below plots three possible Pulse segments against two dimensions: market size and how acute the pain point is. Read the book for tips on quadrant selection and not that your initial market doesn't have to be your forever market.

```mermaid
quadrantChart
    title Segment Evaluation
    x-axis Low Market Size --> High Market Size
    y-axis Low Pain --> High Pain
    quadrant-1 Whales
    quadrant-2 Beachhead
    quadrant-3 Distractions
    quadrant-4 Mass Market
    "Athletes" : [0.65, 0.35]
    "Patients" : [0.45, 0.55]
    "Everyday people" : [0.35, 0.85] radius: 14, color: #8B5CF6, stroke-color: #6D28D9, stroke-width: 3px
```
