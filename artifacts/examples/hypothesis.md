## HYPOTHESIS STATEMENT TEMPLATE
We believe [type of user] has a problem with [specific situation]. We think [this feature or change] will [produce this outcome]. We will know we are right when [measurable signal].

## Pulse Example
We believe people who have tried other health apps and stopped using them within a month have a problem with logging feeling like a chore that demands too much accuracy and consistency too soon. We think a weekly summary view that shows simple totals and trends, without requiring perfect daily logging, will make users feel real progress even on weeks they miss a few days. We will know we are right when 40% of users open the weekly summary within 24 hours of it being available after their first week.
