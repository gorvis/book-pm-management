This repo is a companion for the book _Practical Product Management in the Age of AI_ by Guinevere Orvis.

It is not intended to be contributed to directly. Readers and students should create a fork to complete the labs in the book and save it to their own repo.

If you spot an error or broken link, feel free to open an issue, pull requests won't be merged, but the report is genuinely useful.
